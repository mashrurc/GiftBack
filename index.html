Live Price Updating

On supported web retailers, the price of all products updates seamlessly with the post-donation expense.

Check Differences

Hover over any price to see the original cost and donation total, allowing for easy tracking of expenses.

Clean Interface

The prices updates across all locations, from browsing to the final cart, making you forget your own good deeds

One-Click Donation

Easilly access your dashboard and your favorite charity's donation page with one click.

Easy Changes

Do you want to donate somewhere else today? A different amount? Change that with convienient sliders.

Lifetime Totals

See your total, live-updating donation balance and lifetime donations for a sense of self-satisfaction.

Install and Begin in 4 Simple Steps

We all know how hard it is to remember to donate, even if it is always something you wanted to do. GiveBack will change that.

Install

Download the extension and pin it to the toolbar for easy access.

Choose a Pledge

Pick an amount you wish to donate on each online order. Percentage and flat-rate options are available. We reccommend 2%.

Choose a Charity

We support donations to over 50 supported Canadian and worldwide charities, that are trusted and verified by independent groups.

Sit Back and Shop

You are done. Now go to Walmart, Amazon, and other supported retailers and feel good knowing your purchases are making a difference. You can always change your settings in the Dashboard.

Account Settings

Change your name, password, and other vital details in your account settings.

Donations Ledger

Keep track of all your donations through a helpful date-sorted ledger. Important for tax rebate purposes.

Statistics

Helpful graphs and statistics track how much you have donated to each cause, and whether your donations are increasing or decreasing over time.

Easy Access

No long forms, or messy spreadsheets. Everything is hosted on our clean, well-designed web page, one click away from our Chrome extension.

Chrome Extension

The GiftBack extension increases the prices of your purchases to your desire and tracks the excess as a donation to a charity of your choice. Supported on Amazon, Walmart, and many other popular online retailers.

Personalized Dashboard

The dashboard allows you to change your default donation amount (a percentage or flat rate) and the charity you choose to donate to. If you cannot decide, we have provided a list of trustworthy charities.
